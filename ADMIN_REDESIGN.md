## 🎨 **Design Principles Applied**

1. **Consistency** - All elements follow the same design language
2. **Hierarchy** - Clear visual hierarchy with typography and spacing
3. **Whitespace** - Generous padding and margins for readability
4. **Color** - Brand colors used consistently
5. **Typography** - Professional fonts and sizes
6. **Feedback** - Visual feedback on interactions
7. **Accessibility** - Proper contrast ratios
8. **Responsiveness** - Mobile-first approach
